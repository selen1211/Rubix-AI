Business Model

Rubix AI operates on a dual-faceted business model designed to cater to both service providers and clients effectively. Our platform is built on the principles of transparency, quality, and convenience, ensuring a win-win situation for everyone involved.

For Service Providers

Professionals on Rubix AI, leveraging the Web3 framework, benefit from a commission-based model, where a nominal fee is charged only on successfully completed projects. This approach incentivizes high-quality service delivery, as professionals grow their reputation on the platform and secure more opportunities within the Web3 ecosystem. Additionally, Rubix AI offers various subscription plans tailored to service providers seeking enhanced visibility and features. These plans are designed to utilize the advantages of Web3, ensuring professionals can effectively showcase their skills and expand their client base in a decentralized environment.

For Clients

Clients have complimentary access to explore and engage with professionals on Rubix AI. Service payments are securely processed through the blockchain platform, supported by a satisfaction guarantee policy. This policy ensures clients only pay when completely satisfied with the work. For those in need of regular or specialized services, Rubix AI offers premium plans granting priority access and exclusive benefits, incorporating tokenomics for added value.

Sustainable Growth

Our business model is rooted in sustainability and scalability, ensuring that as Rubix AI expands, so too does the wealth of opportunities and value for our community of professionals and clients. Committed to ongoing enhancement, we actively incorporate community feedback and market insights to refine our platform and services. This approach guarantees that our growth is always in harmony with the evolving needs and aspirations of our community.
